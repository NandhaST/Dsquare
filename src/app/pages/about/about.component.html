DSquare Taxi, the Brand of Outstation Cab Service acts as a cab booking facilitator connecting Cab operators and Riders across the country. We constantly strive to achieve the benchmarks of cost effective, reliability and flexibility which makes DSquare Taxi a trusted brand for all your Intercity travel needs.

Being derived from the term, rekla, DSquare Taxi just follows the simple theme of a Rekla race where time and safety are our prime goals.
